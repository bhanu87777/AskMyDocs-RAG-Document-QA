# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (2026 Edition)

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue **20 days of paid vacation per year**, accruing at 1.67 days per month. Unused PTO of up to **5 days** may be carried over into the next calendar year; anything above 5 days is forfeited on December 31. PTO requests must be submitted at least **two weeks in advance** through the HR portal and approved by your manager.

New employees begin accruing PTO on their first day but may not take vacation during their first **90 days** of employment.

## 2. Sick Leave

Employees receive **10 paid sick days per year**, separate from PTO. Sick leave does not carry over. A doctor's note is required for any sick absence longer than **three consecutive days**.

## 3. Remote Work Policy

Acme operates on a **hybrid model**: employees are expected in the office **Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday**, and may work remotely on Monday and Friday. Fully remote arrangements require VP approval and are reviewed annually.

Remote employees must be reachable on Slack during core hours, which are **10:00 AM to 4:00 PM** in their local time zone.

## 4. Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses are reimbursed within **15 business days** of an approved submission. Receipts are required for any expense over **$25**. The daily meal allowance while traveling is **$60**. Airfare must be booked in economy class unless the flight exceeds **6 hours**, in which case premium economy is permitted.

Home-office stipend: new remote employees receive a one-time **$500** stipend for equipment.

## 5. Health Benefits

Acme covers **80%** of the medical premium for employees and **50%** for dependents. The dental plan is fully covered by the company. Open enrollment occurs every November. The 401(k) plan includes a company match of **100% of the first 4%** of salary contributed.

## 6. Security & Data Handling

All employees must enable **two-factor authentication** on their Acme accounts. Company laptops are encrypted with FileVault or BitLocker. Customer data must never be copied to personal devices. Suspected security incidents must be reported to security@acme.example within **one hour** of discovery.

Passwords must be at least **14 characters** and are rotated every **180 days**.

## 7. Code of Conduct

Acme is committed to a harassment-free workplace. Violations should be reported to hr@acme.example or through the anonymous ethics hotline. Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## 8. Performance Reviews

Formal performance reviews are conducted **twice a year**, in June and December. Ratings range from 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Exceptional). Promotions are considered during the December cycle only.
